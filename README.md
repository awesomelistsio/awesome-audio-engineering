## Foundations & Standards

- [AES (Audio Engineering Society)](https://www.aes.org/) – Professional organization defining audio standards and research.
- [IEC Audio Standards](https://www.iec.ch/dyn/www/f?p=103:85:0::::FSP_LANG_ID:25) – International standards for audio equipment and measurement.
- [ITU-R Audio Recommendations](https://www.itu.int/rec/R-REC-BS/en) – Broadcast and audio quality standards.
- [Nyquist–Shannon Sampling Theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nyquist%E2%80%93Shannon_sampling_theorem) – Fundamental principle of digital audio sampling.
- [Loudness Units (LUFS)](https://tech.ebu.ch/loudness) – Standardized loudness measurement for broadcast and streaming.

## Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs)

- [Pro Tools](https://www.avid.com/pro-tools) – Industry-standard DAW for recording, editing, and mixing.
- [Logic Pro](https://www.apple.com/logic-pro/) – Professional DAW for music production on macOS.
- [Ableton Live](https://www.ableton.com/) – Performance-oriented DAW for electronic music and live sets.
- [Reaper](https://www.reaper.fm/) – Lightweight, highly customizable DAW used in studios and post-production.
- [Cubase](https://www.steinberg.net/cubase/) – Full-featured DAW with strong MIDI and scoring tools.
- [Studio One](https://www.presonus.com/products/Studio-One) – Modern DAW with integrated mastering workflow.
- [Ardour](https://ardour.org/) – Open-source DAW for Linux, macOS, and Windows.

## Audio Plugins & Formats

- [VST](https://www.steinberg.net/developers/) – Widely used plugin standard for audio effects and instruments.
- [Audio Units (AU)](https://developer.apple.com/audio-units/) – Apple’s native plugin format for macOS and iOS.
- [AAX](https://www.avid.com/developer/aax) – Plugin format for Pro Tools systems.
- [LV2](https://lv2plug.in/) – Open-source plugin standard used in Linux audio environments.
- [JUCE](https://juce.com/) – C++ framework for building cross-platform audio plugins.
- [CLAP](https://cleveraudio.org/) – Modern open plugin standard designed for performance and extensibility.

## Digital Signal Processing (DSP)

- [The Scientist and Engineer’s Guide to DSP](https://www.dspguide.com/) – Classic free book on DSP fundamentals.
- [Music DSP](https://www.musicdsp.org/) – Collection of DSP algorithms for audio processing.
- [DSPRelated](https://www.dsprelated.com/) – Community resources and articles on DSP theory and practice.
- [FFTW](https://www.fftw.org/) – Fast Fourier Transform library used in audio analysis.
- [SoX](http://sox.sourceforge.net/) – Command-line audio processing utility supporting many formats.

## Audio Programming & Libraries

- [PortAudio](http://www.portaudio.com/) – Cross-platform audio I/O library for real-time audio.
- [RtAudio](https://www.music.mcgill.ca/~gary/rtaudio/) – C++ library for realtime audio input/output.
- [libsndfile](https://libsndfile.github.io/libsndfile/) – Library for reading and writing audio files.
- [PyAudio](https://people.csail.mit.edu/hubert/pyaudio/) – Python bindings for PortAudio.
- [Sounddevice](https://python-sounddevice.readthedocs.io/) – Python module for audio I/O and playback.
- [SuperCollider](https://supercollider.github.io/) – Platform for audio synthesis and algorithmic composition.

## Recording, Mixing & Mastering Tools

- [iZotope RX](https://www.izotope.com/en/products/rx.html) – Advanced audio repair and restoration suite.
- [iZotope Ozone](https://www.izotope.com/en/products/ozone.html) – Mastering software with AI-assisted tools.
- [FabFilter](https://www.fabfilter.com/) – Professional EQ, compression, and mixing plugins.
- [Waves Audio](https://www.waves.com/) – Extensive collection of studio and live audio plugins.
- [Slate Digital](https://slatedigital.com/) – Analog-modeled plugins and virtual mixing systems.
- [TDR Nova](https://www.tokyodawn.net/tdr-nova/) – Free dynamic EQ plugin widely used in mixing.

## Live Sound & Broadcasting

- [Dante Audio](https://www.audinate.com/products/dante-platform) – Audio-over-IP networking technology for live sound.
- [AES67](https://www.aes.org/publications/standards/search.cfm?docID=96) – Interoperability standard for networked audio.
- [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/) – Open-source software for live streaming and broadcasting.
- [QLab](https://qlab.app/) – Audio, video, and show control software for live performances.
- [Mixing Station](https://mixingstation.app/) – Remote control app for digital mixing consoles.

## Acoustics & Room Analysis

- [Room EQ Wizard (REW)](https://www.roomeqwizard.com/) – Acoustic measurement and room analysis software.
- [Acoustic Treatment Guide](https://ethanwiner.com/acoustics.html) – Practical guide to studio acoustics and treatment.
- [EASE](https://www.ada-acoustics.com/ease/) – Acoustic modeling software for venues and sound systems.
- [CATT-Acoustic](https://www.catt.se/) – Room acoustics prediction and simulation tool.
- [OpenAIR](https://www.openairlib.net/) – Open database of room impulse responses.

## Audio Measurement & Analysis

- [SMAART](https://www.rationalacoustics.com/smaart/) – Real-time audio measurement and analysis for live sound.
- [SpectraFoo](https://www.metric-halo.com/products/software/spectrafoo) – Professional audio analysis tool.
- [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) – Open-source audio editor with analysis features.
- [WaveSurfer](https://www.speech.kth.se/wavesurfer/) – Tool for sound visualization and speech analysis.
- [Praat](https://www.fon.hum.uva.nl/praat/) – Software for phonetic analysis of speech.

## Hardware & Interfaces

- [Universal Audio](https://www.uaudio.com/) – Audio interfaces and DSP-powered plugins.
- [RME Audio](https://www.rme-audio.de/) – High-end audio interfaces and converters.
- [MOTU](https://motu.com/) – Audio interfaces and AVB networking solutions.
